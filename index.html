What this audit does

This audit evaluates whether your customer-facing workflows can be safely completed when AI is involved — or when workflows are already subject to strict completion, compliance, or regulatory constraints.

It focuses on completion risk, not intent detection; operational failure modes, not model quality; and regulatory and liability exposure, not AI maturity.

What this is not

  • A security audit
  • An AI readiness or maturity assessment
  • A product pitch
  • A generic compliance checklist

There is no score to optimize for and no demo at the end.

Why this matters

AI is increasingly used to initiate workflows — starting transactions, requesting documents, triggering account changes, or guiding customers through regulated actions.

But once a workflow requires identity verification, disclosures or signatures, exact step order, system-of-record writes, or irreversible state changes, most AI-driven systems still break, escalate to humans, or fail silently.

That gap — between initiation and safe completion — is where real risk lives.

Who this is for

Relevant if you are responsible for customer-facing digital workflows, regulated or high-stakes processes, operational reliability, compliance, or AI-enabled CX and automation.

It applies whether AI already touches your workflows, or AI does not yet touch them but completion is critical or regulated.

If none of that applies, the audit will tell you that directly.

What you'll get

  • A clear classification of your workflow risk
  • An explanation of why that risk exists
  • Practical guidance on the level of completion control required

If there's nothing to address, you'll be told that directly.

How this works
